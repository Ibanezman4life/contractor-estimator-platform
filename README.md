\# Contractor Estimator Platform



Multi-company white-label contractor estimating platform.



\## Goal



Build a SaaS platform that allows contractors to:



\- create an account

\- complete company onboarding

\- upload their logo

\- configure pricing rules

\- configure quote templates

\- upload job photos

\- generate AI-assisted estimates

\- search suppliers based on job location

\- compare results by cost, distance, shipping, and speed

\- generate branded PDF quotes for their own company



\## Product Direction



This is not a single-company quote app.



This is a multi-company product with:



\- authentication

\- onboarding

\- company settings

\- pricing engine

\- template engine

\- AI scope engine

\- supplier sourcing engine

\- PDF output engine
